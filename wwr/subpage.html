This site focuses on the YSA (Young Single Adults) of Kpong Ghana District and talks about their Gathering place program and all the fun trips organized by the leadership. Staying up to date with this site brings you all the current affairs in the district which are YSA related. You will also receive updates on all past and upcoming events organized by the YSA.

Past Events

Young single adults and their friends love to gather and participate in wholesome activities and experiences that bring them closer to one another and to God. The Gathering place is one program that has made that possible. A gathering place is a designated location (in an existing meetinghouse or institute building) for young single adults and their friends to gather and participate in one or more activities such as; Religious education and gospel learning, Service and social activities, and Self-reliance and educational opportunities. The YSA Planned a jogging exercise, after the jogging, they gathered at the district center to clean and then spent the rest of time doing aerobics and having fun. It was so a memorable experince for all the joy of that day still linger in out hearts and minds till this day.
