LDS KPONG GHANA DISTRICT

“And also, that he shall continue in writing and making a history of all the important things which he shall observe and know concerning my church;” “…… writing, copying, selecting, and obtaining all things which shall be for the good of the church, and for the rising generations that shall grow up on the land of Zion, to possess it from generation to generation, forever and ever. Amen.” (D&C 69:3, 8) “What will announce you is what you focus on”. The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints as part of its mandate to spread all over the world and take the Gospel of Jesus Christ to every son and daughter of God, has created many units across the world. The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints; commonly referred to us LDS has many units in Ghana and for the sake of this site. I will talk about the units put together and called as a district in Kpong Ghana.

The total membership of Kpong Ghana District is 1,692, of which the average sacrament attendance as at the end of fourth quarter of 2022 was 617. Out of the total membership of 1,692, we have; 643 men and 609 women, with 181 young men and 136 young women. There were 1.064 households and 1,271 adults; of which 365 were Single Adults. Within the last twelve months, there were 128 recent converts. Coming to the site of “Kpong YSA”, one should be ready to learn about the history of LDS Kpong district and especially the YSA.

Young Single Adults: simply abbreviated as YSA is a group of young individuals ages 18-30 years who are not legally married. This site focuses on the YSA of Kpong Ghana District and talks about their Gathering place program and all the fun trips organized by the leadership. Staying up to date with this site brings you all the current affairs in the district which are YSA related. You will also receive updates on all past and upcoming events organized by the YSA.
